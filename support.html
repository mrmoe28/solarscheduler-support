‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new solar installation job?

Go to the Jobs section and tap the "+" button. Fill in the customer information, system size, and project details. You can also set the installation date and assign crew members.

Can I track equipment inventory?

Yes! The Equipment section allows you to track solar panels, inverters, and other equipment. You can monitor inventory levels, set low stock alerts, and manage vendor information.

How do I schedule installations?

Use the Calendar section to view and schedule installations. You can see your crew's availability, weather conditions, and optimize your installation schedule.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all your data is stored locally on your device. SolarScheduler doesn't require an internet connection for core functionality, ensuring your business data remains private and secure.

Do I need a subscription?

No subscription required! SolarScheduler is a one-time purchase with all features included. Your data is stored locally on your device.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your project data, customer information, and reports for backup or analysis purposes.

üîß Troubleshooting

Try restarting your device and ensuring you have the latest version of iOS. If the problem persists, contact support with your device model and iOS version.

Make sure your device has sufficient storage space. If you're still having issues, try force-closing the app and reopening it.

Close other apps running in the background to free up memory. Restart your device if performance issues persist.
